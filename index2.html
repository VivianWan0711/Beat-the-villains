Stage_0:place blockClick a cell and type b to place a block

Stage_1:place red block on a cell and type one of the numbers 1 to 4

You can place at most 3 red blocks with number1,

at most 2 red blocks with number2, at most 2 red

blocks with number3, at most 1 red block with number4

Stage_2:place black block click on a cell and type one of the numbers 1 to 4

you can place at most 3 black blocks with number1,

at most 2 black blocks with number2, at most 2 black

blocks with number3, at most 1 black block with number4

Stage_3:Playing You are the red blocks. Select a red block and type

a,w,s,d to move left,up,down,right.Eat black blocks with number smaller than you!

Avoid being eaten by black blocks with number greater than or equal to you!

Exception: red 1 can eat black 4! You can only move one red block each turn.You can't move onto a block.
